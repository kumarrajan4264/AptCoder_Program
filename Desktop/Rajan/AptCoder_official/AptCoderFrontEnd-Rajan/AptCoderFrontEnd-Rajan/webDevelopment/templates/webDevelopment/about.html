Why Aptcoder

AptCoder is a new innovative website coming to the world of new-age kids who are the future of tomorrow. Let’s start with the fact that why we are coming into the market and what is the main reason behind our project.


In today’s time of smartphones and mobile apps, every kid knows how to use electronic gadgets and make proper use of it while studying. But nobody has any idea how these apps are made and what coding technology is used. This is because of the lack of learning which is not there for kids at an early age. We are committed to creating an online classroom atmosphere that is stimulating and encouraging to students. We have a team of dedicated, resourceful, and goal-driven professional educators with solid commitments to the social and academic growth of every child. If it was introduced back at those times when it was very much required, then the situation would be different for many young generations. So this lack of knowledge which was not given earlier will be possible now through our medium. We as Aptcoder are coming into the market as an online coding platform where kids from kindergarten to 12th class can be able to learn and understand what coding is all about.


Concept Building

The building process for children can only be strong if their base is made from the starting phase of their journey of learning. Aptcoder will give a platform where every child will get to learn and understand the concept of coding from scratch to an advanced level. Professional experts will teach from basic languages which will help them to understand concepts. Teachers will give live online classes according to the level selected by the particular students. We give full assurance that our platform will make your child reach a high height in the field of coding.


Career Nurturing

Research shows that by 2030, most of the jobs will be taken by Artificial Intelligence or Machine Learning. Almost all the industries, in some way, integrate coding to run their businesses. The study also suggests that most of the brain development of a child starts at an early age, which brings logical thinking and aptitude in their daily life prospects. This will not only nurture their career in the long run but also helps them in getting ready with an early age professional portfolio to meet the future demand and churn their brain to become a successful entrepreneur. From the smallest electrical switch to massive rockets, every industry is now looking for a creative skillset, which will effectively improve its profits. Learning at a young age will help them to understand, solve their problems, develop logical thinking, grab future technologies, and most importantly to be a job provider instead of a job seeker.


Global Opportunities

With the introduction of industry4.0 and internet access to almost everyone, they open a gate to a vast set of opportunities. This will help AptCoder alumnus to interact with another alumnus to participate in various learning activities, such as coding competition, bug finder, etc. Future companies will mainly look for potential candidates having the skills required for their growth. While learning these concepts at this early stage may pace up kids with future demand. New technologies bring demand for skilled assets in every field, be its healthcare, fabrication, manufacturing, aviation, IT, or any industry. More than being a kid learning coding and logical thinking, skills, and knowledge gained during AptCoder sessions will help your kids to become a part of this global change that you want to see.



How will AptCoder help ??


Courses & Seminars

We will help our students first by guiding them on how to get started with the training process. Professional mentors will be there to provide proper guidance and also give trail classes of the course they select. Kids of today’s time are using smartphones and apps so they know which app to use, through interactive and animated videos learning can be done in a fun way. These courses are designed with rigorous R&D done by various top industry professionals, IIT, IIM alumnus, and child psychologists. We understand the regular course load of kids, so to deliver the course effectively, our mentor will tap the progress of the kids and keep them engaged. Our main aim is to connect kids to our platform through various coding competitions, bug finder events, and many other things.


Project based learning

Theoretical knowledge is important to grasp all the concepts of a particular coding language. But AptCoder will focus on both, firstly mentors will clear every doubt and then gives children small assignments related to the topic. So that they can understand the concept thoroughly. Our interactive mentors are engaged to fulfill this gap with daily life projects, tests, and industry-related projects. In every programming language, the course has major time involved in completing several projects, so on completion, kids can develop the skills required for the coming future.


Location NO BAR

We don’t want kids to suffer in terms of time, money, and opportunities. We are spending more to accompany the best interactive mentors at your screen via live classes to bring everything closer. As we want kids to learn and implement this knowledge by applying it through our online coding community, where regular competitions and events will be organized by expert professionals from Google, Microsoft, Boeing, Airbus, Facebook, etc. With our local and global industry/university partners, we frequently conduct boot camps, career fairs & webinars to keep everyone updated with the current market demand.
