Objectives

Childhood is one of the most productive times for any individual, there is immense learning capacity, an ability of understand things at a faster pace given proper directions. It is the time when we learn how to walk, play games, learn social behaviors. Therefore the kind of environment and opportunities presented to us in this period creates a larger impact on overall life ahead. The daily chores and surrounding environment later on turn into various habits such as food habits, social behavior, study habits, etc. That make the basis of daily living for us, for rest of our lives. Given proper attention this time could be turned into an opportunity to create curiosity within young minds for learning skills that could be useful to them throughout life.


Why Coding?

In today's world computers and technology have become part of our everyday lives and our involvement with digital world is growing everyday. To understand it better one must know the basics of how things work in digital world and therefore these precious years in which we have immense grasping power can be used for learning some basic skills of digital world and get ahead of everyone.
Kids, they entertain every new thing with full curiosity and they don't know what's right and what's not so often we have to spoon fed them proper nutrition that could nurture their body and proper skills that could nurture their brain. And in this case it becomes parent's responsibility to armament them with a proper skill set.
